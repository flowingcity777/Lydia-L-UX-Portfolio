About

A thoughtful builder with a human-centered lens

I’m interested in how products affect people’s behavior and emotions, not just whether they function. UX design allows me to think about how to make experiences clearer, calmer, and more supportive, especially in areas where people may feel uncertainty or pressure.

My projects often sit at the intersection of clarity, creativity, and emotional comfort. I’m especially drawn to experiences that help people make, learn, heal, or navigate uncertainty more easily.

Currently focused on

  • Building a stronger UX portfolio with intentional case studies
  • Exploring product ideas that feel humane rather than addictive
  • Improving frontend craft while staying grounded in user needs
